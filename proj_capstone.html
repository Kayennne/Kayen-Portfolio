Project Type: Group (4)


Role: 3D Modelling, UI Designer, Marketing

Timeline: 18 Weeks


Tools: Maya, Blender, Zbrush, Substance Painter, Procreate, Illustrator, Unity

Overview

This is the final year project that is still ongoing


In this project, we are able to create any game we want. This game is called Vitale's Valor, it is a stylised medieval RPG game, where I am in charge of creating environmental assets and UI elements, take a look at the images below to see my progress!! In this project, I focus on making my models modular and having shared trim sheet, in order to optimise performance

If you are interested to follow up with the progress of this project, follow out instagram page @humongousdwarfs

Background

At the start, our main character, Delilah, fights a Big Boss, who is attempting to takeover the world, in which she gets defeated. But she was then saved by her sword and got teleported into another dimension

When transported to this new dimension, a spirit comes out of the sword to explain that he is a deity that created this sword for dire situations, and explains to the MC that she has to travel through 3 realms, Ice, Fire and Nature, to collect a total of 3 artefacts, in order to gain more power, and to fight the Big Boss again
