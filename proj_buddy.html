Buddy

UI/UX Design

Project Type: Individual


Role: UI/UX Design, Illustrating

Timeline: 5 Weeks


Tools: Figma, Illustrator

Overview

Task: To Design a mobile app & a smart watch prototype using Figma, with heavy consideration for UX methodologies. The design should be made with the consideration of the pandemic period, and should have the potential to stay on even after COVID-19


In this project, I had to put heavy consideration into the audience I was designing for, which included planning for the colour scheme, theme, and the features the app required. I have also made a proposal with a more in depth explanation on the design and features, click here for more!

*All illustrations are done by me

Background

Welcome to Buddy! A enhancement of the app called "Study Bunny", where you study with a buddy, and get to plan your study schedule, listen to chill music, time yourself, view your study statistics, and more! This app aims to help users feel more light-hearted and comfortable with studying during the pandemic
