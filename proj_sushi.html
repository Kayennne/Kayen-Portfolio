Oh So Sushi

Card Game

Project Type: Group - 4 People


Role: Prototyper, Game Designer, Graphic Designer

Timeline: 7 Weeks


Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Procreate

Overview

Task: You are a small game design firm that wants to publish its first card game. As a team, you need to come up with a prototype to pitch to the toy fairs in December. If the publishers like your game, they will strike a deal with you to produce the game and distribute it around the world.


This was a project that required constant prototyping and testing, ideating and improving, over and over again, until the game was good. It put a test to our game mechanic skills and thinking. Also having to think about how we can make the game affect the player emotionally and instincts. In this project, I designed the rulebook and sushi menu. And also developed the game mechanics and idea.

Click here to view the pitch for our game!

Background

Every player is a chef at a sushi restaurant! But in order to open the sushi restaurant, the chef needs ingredients and items on his menu. The chefs have to earn themselves these different popular sushi ingredients and build their sushi to add to the menu. At the same time take down your competitors and have the best sushi restaurant on the street!
