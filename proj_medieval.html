The Forgotten Eight

3D Modular Modelling

Project Type: Individual


Role: Sculpting, Modelling, Texturing

Timeline: 5 Weeks


Tools: ZBrush, Maya, Substance Painter, Unity

Overview

Task: Using modular asset building concept, create a medieval interior setup with a Lshape corridor leading towards a round table hall with a round table and a floating crystal


The aim of the project was to expose myself to as many different techniques used in the real modelling industry, at a beginner level. Using appropriate modelling and texturing techniques such as modular building, and texturing with texture atlas and trim sheets. I also learned a few basics of sculpting in ZBrush, and how to quad draw in maya to reduce the face count of the models I made in ZBrush. Click here for a breakdown on the hero assets!

Background

Deep under the land, hides a small room with eight chairs. Eight chairs belonging the ones most refer to The Forgotten Eight. As they traverse the land high and low with only one goal in mind - to find an amulet that was believed to have been gifted by a dearly worshipped God, Ashruka. Through their journey, The Eight would earn themselves a remarkably notorious reputation, leaving no mercy on their trail and to those that cross their path.

Though years later, word about them has become sparse. No one seemed to know their whereabouts. Until a man and his sea crew stumbled upon eight skulls laid next to a huge rock on a coastline, arranged nicely in a straight line. One of the skulls held a necklace within its mouth. The eight skulls has yet to be identified, but with most knowing the motives of The Eight, many assumed that they had unfortunately passed on during their search for the amulet. The Eight's skulls are now placed back in the small room and left alone to forgotten, but the mystery of their deaths has not
