The Loop

3D Modular Modelling

Project Type: Individual


Role: Modelling, Texturing, Visual Effects

Timeline: 5 Weeks


Tools: Maya, Substance Painter, Unity

Overview

Task: to build a “sci-fi corridor” environment consisting of a central hero area with a L-shaped junction. At the end of the pathway is a door that should teleport you back to the start of the hallway, creating a never ending loop effect.


This project allowed me to try my hands on visual effect elements, and expand my 3D Modelling skills that I have previously acquired in "The Forgotten Eight" project. This time with a better understanding of modular building. In this project, I have modelled every wall component seperately, thus giving me a variaty of patterns to play with when piecing them together. For a more visual breakdown of the design, click here

Background

"Again. Again. And Again. Walking in loops in this place, it seems there is no way out of this place. All I see is a door, but everytime I walk through it, I end up in the same place again. This place is suffocating. I can see the space outside through the large windows, freedom feels so close to me, yet I'm trapped in this endless cycle. I sometimes wonder if this is all just an illusion..."
