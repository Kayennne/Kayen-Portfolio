My Life

Events I have done in Ngee Ann Poly!

I'm a very outgoing person, I love going for events and making new friends! My life in Ngee Ann has been enriched with such fun-filled adventures and activities, and I want to share that with you!

2024

Freshman Orientation Camp

11-12 April 2024

As of AY23/24, I am the Vice-President of Events in ICT Society, and for FOP 2024, I am the overall-in-charge of the event along woth 2 other leaders. I had lots of fun planning the event together with the EXCO team, and felt proud when I saw my OGLs bond with each other so quickly. I also designed the class flags for each clan.

Open House

Open house 2024 was the first event I was in charge of after being appointed Vice-President of Events. It was my first huge step to becoming a better leader. Being more independant and having to manage more behind the scene stuff, it was definitely a good experience as my first planning event. Dance 4 Fun

19 April 2024

This was a tough event, given a tight timeline of 1 week, to gather freshmen to join us in the dance competition and teach them the choreography (we had 30 sign ups but more than 15 dropped out D:). But nonetheless, my team and I pulled through, including the wonderful freshies that stayed with us till the end. We have a very fun but tiring week , spending almost everyday with one another practicing the dance. Everyone bonded well, and I was so amazed by how the freshies connected with one another so quickly. I'm so grateful for this team!

Community Service @ Yong-en Active Hub (YEAH!)

21 February 2024

In this little community service event we had, we did arts and crafts with the elderly. We were all assigned to one elderly, and I met this wonderful lady that shared with me about her family and her children, and her thoughts on our modern generation. I think it was quite refreshing to listen to perspectives of someone from a different generation, because the insights they give is different from what you hear from your peers. Also, she is a really good painter!! She has paintings hanging on the walls of the centre.

2023

Overseas Immersion Programme - ShenZhen

11-23 September 2023

This was a school exchange trip to ShenZhen for 12 days, and it was one of the most fun I've had, along with great exposure to the school culture there. I made a bunch of friends from China whom we still communicate with till this day. I learn things like business technology lessons, making mooncakes, we had educational visits to cultural sites, played group bonding games, and so much more!

Youth Expedition Project - Klang

13-24 March 2023

In this trip, our aim was to teacher refugee students in Klang, more about how to implement technology into education, and use it in the workforce. This was the first overseas trip I went to in Poly, and I would love to say that I've made lifelong friends here. 12 days, 22 people, everyone lived together in a Shalom, we ate together, played card games together. We made lots of great memories together on this trip. In addition, we made friends with the refugee students there. Even though we were supposed to be seen as "teachers", we were the ones that learnt from them. In fact, they were more knowledgable than us, one of the students had his own Youtube channel where he shares internet hacks, and another who is fluent in the python coding language. This trip was really an educational one

Freshman Orientation Camp

12-14 April 2023

I love Freshman Orientation Camps! I did the same in secondary school, and was so glad when I got the chance to do it again in Poly. I really loved the class I was assigned to, they all bonded and created their own friend groups really easily even without me having to intervene. Moreover, my clan, Centaur, was so fun to be with, we bonded over training days and I got to know them better, it made me really glad to work with these bunch!
