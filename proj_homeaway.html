Overview

Task: To design a 3D virtual level based on a scenario, with careful consideration to User Experience


In this project, I expanded on the coding skills I learned in my first C# coding project, Bluey's First Date. Some of the requirements were a start and pause menu, 2 different areas, 1 indoor and 1 otudoor, and a collectible system.

Background

You are on your way home from a solo mission on another planet. However, on your way home, the core that powers your ship started to lose energy! You had to conduct an emergency landing and you have crashed on an unknown planet. Find a way to power your core and go back home!
