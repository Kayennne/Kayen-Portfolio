3D Environmental Design

Project Type: Individual


Role: Level Design, VFX and Shader Graph Application

Timeline: 4 Weeks


Tools: Quixel Bridge, Unity, Maya, Substance Painter

Overview

Task: To build an exterior environment for a real-time engine that displays knowledge of modular architectural modelling


This project gave me the opportunity to expand on my creativity, while playing with the rich variety of shader graphs and different effects I can make with it

Background

Task: To Sculpt a humanoid character in ZBrush, quad-draw in Maya, texture in Substance Painter, and rig it in Mixamo


Engaging in this project has helped me broaden my horizon on the modelling industry, through enhancing my abilities to discern intricate features of the human body. I've been able to cultivate a deeper sense of perceptiveness that gave light to the outcome of this project. Aside from the aesthetics, this course has enriched my.
