Heiwa

3D Environmental Design

Project Type: Individual


Role: Level Design, VFX and Shader Graph Application

Timeline: 4 Weeks


Tools: Quixel Bridge, Unity, Maya, Substance Painter

Overview

Task: To build an exterior environment for a real-time engine that displays knowledge of modular architectural modelling.
Theme: Vacation Island


This project gave me the opportunity to expand on my creativity in level design, while playing with the rich variety of shader graphs and different particle effects I can make with it. I managed to learn effects such as the ocean waves, a waterfalf particle system, fireflies, fire, falling petals and moving flags. All which contributed to to the overall aesthetics of the peaceful scene, which mean "Heiwa" in Japanese

Background

What better way to get away from the bustling city than a peaceful stroll up the mountains of Wakayama? A design inspired by the Kumano Nachi Grand Shrine, is a land of greenary, shrines, and a waterfall. This place is the epitome peace, truly a must go for one who desires a sense of tranquility. My love for this Japanese landscape led me to create a land of my own that reminds me of the peace that the mountains of Wakayama holds
